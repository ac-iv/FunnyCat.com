Creating homemade treats for your cat can be a fun way to pamper them with healthy and tasty snacks. These easy recipes are sure to be a hit with your furry friend.

Tuna Bites

Mix canned tuna, an egg, flour, and water; drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake until golden. Cool before serving.

Chicken Jerky

Slice chicken breast thinly, place on a baking sheet, and cook in a low-heat oven until dry. Store in an airtight container.

Catnip Yummies

Combine wheat flour, dried catnip, and canned tuna or chicken for a fragrant treat. Roll out, cut into shapes, and bake.

Remember, treats should only make up a small portion of your cat's diet. Always consult with your vet before introducing new foods to ensure they're safe for your pet.
